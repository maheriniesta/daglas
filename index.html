about us

Fresh, Authentic, Personal Interiors!
With more than 13 years experience in Interior Design

daghlas design

Daghlas Design is something other than an interior design organization, we control structures and mixes from our regular world to make a stylish, down-to-earth, and practical environment that changes a straightforward building or space into a position of bliss for you and your visitors.

We have over 13 years of experience, our designers are prepared to convey their skill to your home or office, explicitly custom fitted to meet your requirements.

Each work is an expert satisfaction, autonomous from the style, size, or place. Every client has their taste and want and we meet them with affection, bliss, and strategy.

how we work

we see interior design as strategy in action, focusing on results.
we help my clients envision a better space, and we help them get there successfully.

01 CONSULTATION

We listen to and understand our clients; this communication helps to facilitate an interior design that often exceeds client expectations.

02 DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

Each interior design project is refined through a process of fluid development with continuous, valuable input from our clients.

03 PRESENTATION

Our interior design concepts, ideas and materials are revealed to our clients in a way that is clearly understood.

04 ORDER & DELIVERY

Everything ordered and delivered on time is essential to us. We handle every detail and we oversee the delivery and installation of each item that we order.
